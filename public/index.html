About

Forming effective study groups over long distances is not easy when taking online classes, especially if students do not take an initiative to establish communication to form study groups.
Therefore, a web application with a virtual chat room that allows students to connect and collaborate similarly to how they would in a study group would be beneficial.

We plan to integrate this chat room into our larger Senior Design project in COMP 490.
